Singing Demos for "High-Quality and Efficient Singing Voice Synthesis based on Modified Fastspeech"

Authors:

Peiling Lu, Jie Wu, Jian Luan



Abstract:

This paper presents a singing voice synthesis system based on modified Fastspeech with high quality and efficiency. Fastspeech network is adopted to predict acoustic features from musical score information in parallel. Considering the speciality of singing, we add a residual connection to model F0 more accurately. Besides, the duration loss of every musical note is taken into account during model training for rhythm enhancement. Final results indicate that its MOS gains over baseline reach 1.30 on sound quality, 1.21 on pronunciation accuracy and 1.43 on naturalness respectively. In A/B tests, new F0 modeling achieves 90.4% preference while new duration predictor achieves 87.3% preference, which reveal overwhelming advantages. On the other hand, the inference efficiency of the proposed network is also outstanding.
